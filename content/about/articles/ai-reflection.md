title: "Article 4: Using AI While Building This Website"
---

I used AI mainly as a helper to guide me through Hugo’s structure, Git commands, and the assignment requirements. It helped me fix errors, organize my folders correctly, and write cleaner descriptions for my pages.

AI was helpful for explanations and examples, but I still had to understand the structure myself. Sometimes the answers didn’t fit my setup exactly, so I had to adjust things on my own.

Overall, AI made the process faster, but the actual decisions and final work were still mine.
